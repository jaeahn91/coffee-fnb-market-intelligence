# Data Sources

## 1. Green Bean Import Data

- Primary source: Korea Customs Service / trade statistics
- Target HS Code: 090111
- Description: Coffee, not roasted, not decaffeinated
- Metrics to collect:
  - Import volume
  - Import value
  - Import unit price
  - Country of origin
- Frequency: Monthly
- Note: May 2026 data may not be finalized yet, so the first report may use the latest available confirmed month.

## 2. International Coffee Price

- Primary source: International Coffee Organization
- Metrics to collect:
  - ICO Composite Indicator Price
  - Colombian Milds
  - Other Milds
  - Brazilian Naturals
  - Robustas
- Frequency: Daily or monthly
- Note: Use monthly averages for the first version.

## 3. FX Data

- Primary source: Bank of Korea ECOS
- Metric to collect:
  - USD/KRW exchange rate
- Frequency: Daily or monthly
- Note: For the first report, monthly average is enough.

## 4. Origin and Supply Risk

- Sources:
  - ICO Coffee Market Report
  - Reuters
  - industry news
- Topics:
  - Brazil weather
  - Vietnam robusta supply
  - Colombia production/export issues
  - Ethiopia logistics/political risk
  - freight and shipping cost

## 5. Domestic F&B / Coffee Market Trend

- Sources:
  - KATI
  - food industry news
  - franchise/cafe market news
- Topics:
  - cafe price changes
  - franchise strategy
  - consumer demand
  - input cost pressure
